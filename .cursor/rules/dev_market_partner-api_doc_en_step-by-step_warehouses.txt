Disable or enable storage
If you want to temporarily remove goods from the showcase, disconnect the warehouse where they are located. To do this, make a requestPOST v2/campaigns/{campaignId}/warehouse/status, where pass the parameterenabledwith the valuefalse. The goods will be hidden from the showcase in 15 minutes.
