Managing goods in the archive
You can archive your products so that they disappear from the storefronts in all stores. You cannot send an item stored in a Market warehouse to the archive.
First, such goods need to be sold or exported.

• To archive products, use a requestPOST v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-mappings/archive. If the items could not be archived, they will be returned in the request response.
