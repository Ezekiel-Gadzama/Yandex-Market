Questions and answers about products
Yandex Market API It allows you to receive questions about products and answers to them, as well as add, edit, and delete store responses.

Get questions about products
Get questions about the seller's products by sending a requestPOST v1/businesses/{businessId}/goods-questions. To return questions about certain products, in the parameterofferIdssend their IDs.

offerIds The results are returned page by page, one page contains no more than 50 questions.
