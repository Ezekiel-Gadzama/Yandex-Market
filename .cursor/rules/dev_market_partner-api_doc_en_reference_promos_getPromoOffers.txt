Getting a list of products that participate or may participate in the promotion
The method is available forall models. Not yet available for Market Yandex Go sellers.

• pricing —Manage prices
• pricing:read-only —View prices
• promotion —Product promotion
• promotion:read-only —View promotion information
• all-methods — Full account management
• all-methods:read-only — View all data
Returns a list of products that participate or may participate in the promotion. The terms of participation in the promotions may change
For example,maxPromoPrice.

maxPromoPrice The set prices will not change. —priceandpromoPrice.
