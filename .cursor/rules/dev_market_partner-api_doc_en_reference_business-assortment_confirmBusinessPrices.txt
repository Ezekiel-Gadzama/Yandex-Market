Removing an item from quarantine based on the price in the cabinet
The method is available forall models.

• pricing —Manage prices
• all-methods — Full account management
Confirms the price of quarantined goods in all stores and removes them from quarantine. A product is quarantined if its price changes too dramatically.How to set up quarantine
To see the list of products that have been quarantined, use a requestPOST v2/businesses/{businessId}/price-quarantine.

⚙️ Limit:10,000 products per minute, no more than 200 products per request

POST
