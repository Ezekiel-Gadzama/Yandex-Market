How to sign integrations
In the seller's account on the Market on the pageAPIs and moduleson the tabQuery logYou can view information on the requests and responses, as well as on the issues related to them. To understand which integration the data belongs to, you need to sign:

• Requeststhat you send to Yandex Market API, — in the header, specify the name of the integration and its version, if any.Format:X-Market-Integration: integration name/version.For example,X-Market-Integration: BitrixorX-Market-Integration: Bitrix/1.0.0.
