Coverage Promotion Report
The method is available forall models. Not yet available for Market Yandex Go sellers.

• promotion —Product promotion
• finance-and-accounting —View financial data and reports
• all-methods — Full account management
Starts the generation of a summary report on the coverage promotion. What kind of report is this:for banners,for push notifications.
You can find out the generation status and get a link to the finished report using a request.GET v2/reports/info/{reportId}.
