Transfer and confirmation of the refund decision
The method is available forall models.

• inventory-and-order-processing —Order processing and inventory
• all-methods — Full account management
Allows you to perform one of the following operations:

• For all models:send a list of refund solutions and confirm them.
For all models:send a list of refund solutions and confirm them.
• For the model DBS:confirm the decision submitted toPOST v2/campaigns/{campaignId}/orders/{orderId}/returns/{returnId}/decision. To do this, use a request without a body.
For the model DBS:confirm the decision submitted toPOST v2/campaigns/{campaignId}/orders/{orderId}/returns/{returnId}/decision. To do this, use a request without a body.

⚙️ Limit:10,000 requests per hour
