Lists of product characteristics by category
The method is available for models:FBY,FBS,ExpressandDBS.

• offers-and-cards-management —Manage products and cards
• offers-and-cards-management:read-only —View products and cards
• all-methods — Full account management
• all-methods:read-only — View all data
Returns a list of characteristics with acceptable values for the specifiedleaf category. The fields in the response define the rules for transmitting characteristics in methods:

• POST v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-mappings/update
• POST v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-cards/update

⚙️ Limit:100 categories per minute
