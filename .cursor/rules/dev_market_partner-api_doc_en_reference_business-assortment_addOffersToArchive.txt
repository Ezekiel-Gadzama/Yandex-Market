Adding products to the archive
The method is available forall models.

• offers-and-cards-management —Manage products and cards
• all-methods — Full account management
Puts the products in the archive. The items stored in the archive are hidden from the display case in all the cabinet stores.
You cannot send an item stored in a Market warehouse to the archive. First, such goods need to be sold or exported.

⚙️ Limit:10,000 products per minute, no more than 200 products per request

Request
POST

https://api.partner.market.yandex.ru/v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-mappings/archive
