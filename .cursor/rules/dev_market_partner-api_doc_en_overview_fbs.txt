List of methods used in the model FBS
By placing products according to the model FBS, you store them in your warehouse, and entrust the delivery of orders to the Market. When a customer places an order, you assemble and pack it, and then either take it to the Market yourself or ship it to a car sent by the Market. Read more about the model inYandex.Market Help for sellers.
