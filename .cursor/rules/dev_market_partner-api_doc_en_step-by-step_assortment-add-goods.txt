Adding and editing products
Using a requestPOST v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-mappings/updateyou can add products to the catalog and transfer:

• theirleaf categorieson the Market and categorical characteristics;
• main features;
• prices of goods in the cabinet.
You can also combine products on the card, edit and delete information about already added products, including prices in the cabinet and product categories.
To find out the source and time of the information update:

Removing products from the catalog
Delete products from the catalog using the API:

• POST v2/businesses/{businessId}/offer-mappings/delete— deletes the specified products from the cabinet catalog
• POST v2/campaigns/{campaignId}/offers/delete— removes the specified products from the assortment of the selected store, does not affect other stores or the availability of the product in the general catalog.
The product can be returned to the catalog Please note: the product will be returned to the catalog again if:

• you are sending the product via the API
• you are using an external integrator solution that synchronizes the product with the Market
• You transfer the goods via the YML file and the synchronization starts.
• you upload the product manually via Excel
• You have enabled data synchronization with external platforms. Ozon/WB in the merchant's personal account
